How It Works Our Story

Our Origin

ON AIR Foundation was born from personal experiences in Colonia Federal, Entre Ríos, where limited educational infrastructure showed how birthplace can define opportunity.

From Virginia, USA, we work to ensure children ages 6–12 gain access to tools that remove geographic barriers to learning.

Mission

Empowering rural communities through quality technological and bilingual education.

Vision

A future where the quality of education does not depend on geographical location.

Values

Digital equity, inclusion and a glocal perspective.

Educational Approach

Digital Literacy

Teaching responsible use of technology to prepare students for academic and professional futures.

English Education

English as a gateway to global knowledge and international collaboration.

Implementation Model

  • Individual technological equipment
  • High-speed satellite connectivity
  • Teacher training (hybrid model)
  • Monthly international mentoring

Social Impact

We envision a future without educational limits, where every child in Entre Ríos can grow as a global citizen.
